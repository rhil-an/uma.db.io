Welcome to Umamusume Database This database stores the racing history, records and facts about umamusume, which are based off their real life counterpart.
What is Umamusume? The word "Umamusume" translates to "horsegirl". It is a popular Japanese multimedia franchise by Cygames, a Japanese videogame studio. Umamusume are anime horsegirls that took inspiration from real life Japanese racehorses.
