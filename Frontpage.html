What is Umamusume?

Umamusume (ウマ娘), literally translating to "horse girl" in Japanese, refers to a multimedia franchise by a Japanese company, Cygames. It features humanoid characters with horse ears, tails, and superhuman running abilities, modeled after real-life, famous racehorses. It is a popular, high-grossing mobile game, anime, and manga series focusing on training these characters for competitive racing and entertainment.
